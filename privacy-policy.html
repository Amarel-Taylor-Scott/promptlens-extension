PromptLens Browser Extension — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

This privacy policy explains how the PromptLens browser extension ("the Extension") collects, uses, and protects your information.

What We Collect

Image data you choose to analyze. When you right-click an image and select "Generate Prompt with PromptLens," the Extension sends that specific image to PromptLens servers for analysis. We only process images you explicitly choose to analyze — we never scan, collect, or access any other images or content on pages you visit.

Extension settings. Your preferences (authentication mode, default model selection, behavior toggles) are stored locally in your browser using the browser's built-in storage API and synced across your devices if you use browser sync. These settings never leave your browser except through your browser's own sync mechanism.

Generation history. A list of your recent analyses (share IDs, timestamps, and source page URLs) is stored locally in your browser. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your browsing history
  • We do not track which websites you visit
  • We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide an email address in the Extension settings
  • We do not inject ads, trackers, or analytics scripts into web pages
  • We do not sell, share, or rent any user data to third parties
  • We do not access any images on a page unless you explicitly right-click and choose to analyze one

How Images Are Processed

When you analyze an image:

  1. The Extension fetches the image and sends it to promptlens.polsia.app via a secure HTTPS connection
  2. Our server processes the image using AI models to generate prompts
  3. The generated prompts are stored and displayed on a results page
  4. Uploaded images are processed temporarily and are not retained permanently on our servers

Authentication

The Extension supports three modes:

  • Anonymous: No personal information is collected. Rate-limited to 3 generations per day.
  • Email-linked: If you provide an email address, it is sent to our server to associate your generations with an account. This email is stored on our servers per our web app privacy policy.
  • API Key: If you provide an API key, it is stored locally in your browser and sent as an authorization header with each request. API keys are generated from your account at promptlens.polsia.app.

Permissions

The Extension requests the following browser permissions:

  • contextMenus: To add the "Generate Prompt with PromptLens" option to the right-click menu on images
  • storage: To save your settings and generation history locally in your browser
  • notifications: To show a notification if the on-page toast cannot be displayed (e.g., on restricted browser pages)
  • activeTab: To inject a small toast notification on the current page showing generation progress
  • Access to all websites: Required to fetch images from any website when you right-click them for analysis. The Extension only accesses a website's content when you explicitly trigger an analysis.

Data Security

All communication between the Extension and PromptLens servers uses HTTPS encryption. Your settings and history are stored using your browser's secure storage API.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
